# FDE Field Test English Localization Analysis

## Content Summary

The source is a public FDE capability challenge built from the OneX FDE assessment and training framework. It contains a 12-question potential test plus 200 level-assessment questions covering foundations, delivery decisions, enterprise architecture, governance, scale, value, and risk. Its purpose is to distinguish people who merely know AI terminology from practitioners who can diagnose a business problem, design a controlled solution, and own delivery outcomes.

The English audience includes global AI delivery practitioners, founders, enterprise decision-makers, solutions leaders, and technically fluent candidates. The public experience must feel credible and demanding while remaining explicit that it is not a formal OneX graduation or certification decision.

## Tone & Style

- Native global product-editorial English: sharp, economical, and outcome-oriented.
- Enterprise scenarios should sound like real delivery decisions, not translated classroom questions.
- Wrong options remain plausible enough to test judgment; avoid making distractors cartoonishly careless unless the Chinese source intentionally does so.
- Explanations state the delivery principle and operational consequence in one or two sentences.
- UI language is direct: `Start the Full Assessment`, `Submit Assessment`, `Qualifies for L2`.

## Translation Challenges

- `校准` is a recurring product metaphor but feels clinical in an entry CTA; use assessment, profile, or instincts depending on context.
- `露馅` signals that weak judgment will be exposed under delivery pressure; render as `where your judgment breaks down`, not `reveal the stuffing`.
- `一关关考出来` means level status must be earned sequentially; use `Advanced is a standard you earn, one level at a time.`
- Chinese organization shorthand such as 老板、甲方、一线、口径 must be adapted to sponsor, client, frontline team, and approved policy/position as context requires.
- Certification-oriented junior source items must retain their logic while the public site boundary says that this challenge itself is not the formal certification process.
- Multi-select instructions must preserve exact-match scoring: missing, extra, or incorrect choices receive zero credit.
- All numbers, thresholds, question IDs, answer indices, and module assignments are immutable.
