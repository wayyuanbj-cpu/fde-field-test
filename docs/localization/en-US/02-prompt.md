# FDE Field Test Refined Translation Prompt

Rewrite the OneX FDE Field Test into publication-quality global English as if a native enterprise AI product team authored it from scratch.

## Audience and purpose

Write for AI delivery practitioners, founders, enterprise decision-makers, solution leaders, and candidates who already understand common AI concepts. The experience must assess whether someone can connect business evidence, technical design, operating controls, and accountable delivery outcomes.

## Voice

- Sharp, credible, concise, and specific.
- Prefer active voice and concrete operational language.
- Preserve the pressure and tradeoffs of real enterprise delivery.
- Avoid Chinese syntax, inflated consulting language, generic AI hype, and literal idioms.
- Do not simplify away risk, authority, evidence, adoption, or acceptance criteria.

## Fixed terms

- FDE → FDE; expand as Field Deployment Engineer on first substantial occurrence.
- 业务诊断 → business diagnosis.
- 场景判断 → use-case judgment.
- 方案构建 → solution design.
- 风险边界 → risk boundaries or operational controls, according to context.
- 推进交付 → delivery execution or delivery ownership.
- 完整挑战 → full assessment.
- 随机模拟 → practice set.
- 晋级 → qualify for the next level.
- 传播型 FDE 能力挑战 → public FDE capability challenge.
- 灵根 → instincts only in the CTA `Test Your FDE Instincts`.

## Immutable assessment contract

For every question, preserve its ID, level, module, type, option count, answer indices, scoring values, and tested capability. Preserve the reason each distractor is wrong. Do not add claims that the public result is a formal graduation, certification, or proof of real-project performance.

## Quality checks

The final English must contain no user-facing Chinese, no mistranslated idioms, no Europeanized or Chinese-shaped sentence structures, and no terminology drift across the 12/100/60/40 banks. Multiple-choice explanations must remain compatible with exact-match scoring.
