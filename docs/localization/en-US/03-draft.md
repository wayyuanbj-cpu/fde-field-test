# FDE Field Test English Draft

The complete first English draft is implemented in the locale source files because question IDs, answer arrays, and option order must remain executable and machine-validated:

- `locales/en-US/quick-question-data.js` — 12 quick scenarios
- `locales/en-US/junior-question-data.js` — 100 foundation questions
- `locales/en-US/intermediate-question-data.js` — 60 applied-delivery scenarios
- `locales/en-US/advanced-question-data.js` — 40 complex-decision scenarios
- `locales/en-US.js` — levels, modules, result profiles, and editorial guidance

## Draft anchors

- Hero: `Could You Actually Ship as an FDE?`
- Deck: `Knowing the tools is not the same as owning the outcome.`
- CTA: `Test Your FDE Instincts`
- Progression: `Advanced is a standard you earn, one level at a time.`
- Public boundary: `This result comes from a public FDE capability challenge. It is not a formal graduation, certification, or determination of real-project capability.`

The executable draft is the full translation artifact; this manifest avoids maintaining a second copy that could drift from the tested assessment.
